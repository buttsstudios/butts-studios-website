Project Overview

Influencer Marketing Platform

Runwae is a comprehensive influencer marketing platform connecting brands with content creators. Butts Studios built the complete web application from concept to launch.

Creator Marketplace

Browse and discover influencers filtered by niche, audience size, and engagement metrics. Detailed creator profiles with portfolio showcase.

Gig Management

End-to-end campaign workflow from proposal to delivery. Milestone tracking, revision requests, and approval workflows.

Secure Payments

Integrated Stripe payments with escrow functionality. Secure payouts for creators and seamless billing for brands.

Messaging System

Real-time messaging between brands and creators. Thread organization and notification management.

Dashboard & Analytics

Comprehensive analytics for campaign performance tracking. Revenue dashboards for creators and conversion metrics for brands.

Account Management

User profiles, settings, verification system, and account security features for both brands and creators.
