Project Overview

Music Industry Platform

WHAM is a comprehensive music label platform featuring artist album releases, merchandise e-commerce, and event management. Butts Studios built the complete digital presence.

Album Releases

Dynamic album pages with track listings, streaming integration, and purchase options. Artists include 50 cal, Denim, Dirt Road, Eleven11, Evolution, and more.

Merchandise Shop

Full e-commerce functionality with product catalog, shopping cart, and checkout. Physical merchandise and digital releases.

Event Management

Event listings, ticketing information, and event details pages. Keep fans updated on tours and appearances.

Artist Profiles

Individual artist pages showcasing their discography, bio, and latest releases with cohesive branding.
